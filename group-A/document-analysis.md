## 1. Project Overview

**Project Title:** Levantamiento Multidisciplinario - Dársena COTECMAR  
**Approval Date:** 08 May 2026  
**Field Work Date:** 16 February 2026  
**Report Date:** 15 April 2026 (M2-FOR-072)  
**Director:** Capitán de Coberta ÁLVAREZ ORDUZ OMAR SEBASTIÁN  
**Institution:** Escuela Naval de Cadetes "Almirante Padilla"  

The project is a hydrographic survey of the COTECMAR shipyard dock (dársena) at Bocagrande, Cartagena, using combined multibeam and single-beam echosounder technology. The survey aims to verify rail alignment and measure sediment thickness for vessel maneuvering operations.

## 5. Objectives

### 5.1 General Objective
"Realizar un levantamiento hidrográfico en la dársena de COTECMAR Bocagrande empleando tecnología multihaz para verificar la alineación de los rieles y las profundidades, y ecosonda monohaz de doble frecuencia para determinar el espesor de la capa de sedimentación."

### 5.2 Specific Objectives
1. Collect multibeam hydrographic data over 4,889.28 meters to verify rail alignment for vessel maneuvering
2. Collect single-beam dual-frequency data over 2,352.04 meters to verify sediment layer thickness
3. Generate digital terrain models, plans, final sheets, and reports
4. Process collected information to produce a final survey sheet
5. Produce digital photographic archive for nautical chart support
